## Benefits

Huge cost savings. Commercial digital signage displays cost $800-2000 each. With BYOD, you spend $0 on hardware.

## How It Works

Connect your screen to a media player or streaming stick. Install software. Start displaying content.

That's it!

Sign up for a digital signage platform. Many offer free trials.

Once you're happy, scale to more screens.
